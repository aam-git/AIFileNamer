# AIFileNamer - Marketing Document

## 🎯 Elevator Pitch
Stop wasting hours manually opening files, guessing what they are, and renaming them. **AIFileNamer** is your fully autonomous desktop assistant that actually *reads* your text, code, and image files, intelligently deduces their context, and renames them into pristine, searchable, and logically organised structures. Bring your own API keys or run it entirely securely and offline on your own hardware.

## 💡 Value Proposition
- **Extreme Time Savings:** Turns days of manual digital spring cleaning into a completely hands-free, autonomous background process.
- **Flawless Searchability:** Wave goodbye to "IMG_019283.jpg" and "Untitled(3).txt". Say hello to `q4_marketing_campaign_graph.jpg` and `database_connection_utility.js`.
- **Absolute Privacy:** For sensitive legal, medical, or proprietary corporate data, users can seamlessly utilise local, open-source AI models (via LM-Studio, Llama.cpp, Jan, GPT4All, or Ollama) guaranteeing their data never leaves their machine.
- **Total Flexibility:** Power users maintain ultimate control with adjustable AI batch limits, processing delays to preserve GPU longevity, specific file extension toggles, and fully customisable AI prompts.

## 👥 Target Audience
- **Photographers & Designers:** Sorting through thousands of cryptically named exports, proofs, PSDs, HEIC photos, and design assets.
- **Business Professionals & Students:** Organising countless PDFs, Word documents (`.docx`, `.doc`), Excel spreadsheets (`.xlsx`, `.xls`), and PowerPoint presentations.
- **Software Engineers & IT Professionals:** Quickly making sense of massive dump files, legacy codebase migrations, or disorganised log folders.
- **Data Analysts & Researchers:** Wrangling messy `.csv`, `.json`, and raw data dumps into semantic structures.
- **Digital Hoarders:** Everyday users trying to reclaim their downloads folder and personal archives.

## 📈 Key Selling Points (KSPs)

### 1. Context-Aware Intelligence
AIFileNamer doesn't just look at creation dates or metadata tags—it actually natively parses text, code snippets, and even *reads* the visual contents of images to derive the absolute best descriptive filename.

### 2. Auto-Run Background Magic
With its robust directory-watching capabilities, simply check the "Auto-Run" box, minimize the app, and drag files into your folder from your OS. AIFileNamer will silently ingest, process, rename, and sort them on the fly.

### 3. Bring Your Own Brain
We don't lock you into a proprietary subscription ecosystem. You provide the brain: whether it's cloud providers like OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, DeepSeek, Google AI Studio, and OpenRouter, or local models running securely on your own hardware via LM-Studio, Llama.cpp, Jan, GPT4All, and Ollama.

### 4. Advanced Directory Sorting
Why stop at renaming? Enable "Suggest Folders", provide the AI with a base schema, and watch it automatically build deep, logical sub-directory structures to categorise your data automatically.

### 5. Multi-Scanner Workspaces
Manage multiple workflows effortlessly. Create dedicated, independent "scanners" for different projects—each with its own target folder, custom AI prompt, chosen provider, and rate limits. Run them all simultaneously from a sleek, unified dashboard without breaking a sweat.
